About WURLD

WURLD is a environmental impact app geared at providing a way to track one's positive contributions to the environment and to engage in friendly competition with others in the surrounding area. It is meant to provide users with easy ways to track their recycling habits through gamification, and create a community where like-minded individuals can help and inspire each other to make environmentally-conscious decisions.
